As a passionate machine learning researcher, I have spent the past 3 years exploring the vast potential of artificial intelligence. My expertise lies in the research and development of cutting-edge technologies such as object detection, action recognition, object tracking, and statistical machine learning modeling. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from [Gujarat Technological University](https://www.gtu.ac.in/), which provided me with a solid foundation in the field. During my time at university, I also collaborated with [Prof. Dr. Arnav Bhavsar](https://iitmandi.irins.org/profile/113329) at IIT Mandi on a research project - Microscopy Image Classification involving Human Protein Atlas. Additionally, I have completed various online courses on deep learning and computer vision, further expanding my knowledge and skill set.

Driven by a desire to leverage the power of technology to solve real-world problems and enhance people's lives, I am committed to advancing the field of artificial intelligence through innovation and hard work.

- Action Recognition
- Multi-modal AI
- Medical Imaging

Besides, I am intrested in Generative AI, reasong and planning.
