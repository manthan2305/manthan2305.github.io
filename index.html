Manthan Solanki

Machine Learning Researcher

As a driven and enthusiastic machine learning researcher, I have dedicated the past three years to unlocking the immense potential of artificial intelligence across diverse industries. My expertise lies in the research and development of cutting-edge technologies such as object detection, action recognition, object tracking, and statistical machine learning modeling. During my time at Sportsseam Inc, I collaborated with Dr. Sunil Kulkarni (CTO) on computer vision prototype.

I graduated from Gujarat Technological University with degree in Computer Engineering, adviced by Prof. Dr. Mita Paunwala. I had the opportunity to work on exciting research under Prof. Dr. Arnav Bhavsar at IIT Mandi on Microscopy Image Classification.

Driven by a desire to leverage the power of technology to solve real-world problems and enhance people’s lives, I am committed to advancing the field of artificial intelligence through innovation and hard work.

My main reseach interests:

  • Intelligent video analytics
  • Action Recognition
  • Multi-modal AI
  • Medical Imaging

Besides, I am intrested in Generative AI, perception, and reasoning.
